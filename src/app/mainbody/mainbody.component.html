  • Improved technologies have had significant impacts on nature, both positive and negative. On one hand, technology has enabled advancements in environmental monitoring, conservation, and restoration efforts. For example, the development of remote sensing technology, such as satellite imagery and drones, has allowed scientists to monitor changes in ecosystems and wildlife populations over time, track the spread of pollution, and plan more effective conservation strategies. Additionally, technology has played a crucial role in the development of renewable energy sources, such as solar panels and wind turbines, which help reduce our reliance on fossil fuels and decrease greenhouse gas emissions.
